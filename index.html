GROUND TYPE POKÉMON

Ground-type Pokémon are charming creatures that embody the power and strength of the earth. They are often depicted as tough and burly with impressive features, like thick hides and imposing claws. With their ability to dig and burrow, they can quickly navigate through caves and mountains or escape danger. Their moves showcase the raw power of the earth, making them a formidable and endearing addition to any trainer's team.
